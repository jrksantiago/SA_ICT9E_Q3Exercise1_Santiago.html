My First Bootstrap Page

HTML

HyperText Markup Language

HTML(HyperText Markup Language) is a Webpage coding application that helps you put text and images to your webpage. HTML is also a great way to communicate with others through webpages. You can also make forms for people to answer your questions in or get to know someone a lot more.

CSS

Cascading Style Sheets

Cascading Style Sheets or CSS is a nice way to organize your HTML webpages. You can also add design to your webpage as much as you like and please. CSS also gives you the freedom to change how your website looks.

Bootstrap

Bootstrap Version 4

Bootstrap is a way for people to see webpages in different devices without consequence whatsoever. It also makes your webpages easier to look and read. Bootstrap also supports all kinds of devices for webpages to fit in.
